Gold Wolf Hunt

Stage 1 to 6 forest rescue template

Control a small golden wolf, hunt rabbits, collect coins, survive forest traps, and rescue your golden friend in stage 3. Rabbits have 200 HP. Each hit deals 50 damage. Defeating one rabbit gives +30 coins.

Stage 1

Easy forest training. Learn movement, jump, and claw attack.

Stage 2

Deeper woods with spikes, more rabbits, and harder platform timing.

Stage 3

Complete the hunt, reach the cage, and rescue your golden friend.

This is a one-file HTML template designed for GitHub Pages. Save it as index.html and upload it to your repo. Stage 4 to 6 include a 2D rain effect.

Controls

Desktop: A / D or Left / Right to move, W or Up to jump, Space to attack. Mobile: use the buttons at the bottom. Attack a rabbit four times to defeat it, then move to the glowing gate. On stage 3, the mission ends at the cage after the rabbit target is complete.

Rabbit HP

200 HP each. Every claw attack deals 50 damage.

Coin Reward

+30 coins when one rabbit is defeated. Damage popups and reward popups are shown in battle.

Lives

You have 3 lives. Hitting spikes or falling resets your position and reduces one life.
